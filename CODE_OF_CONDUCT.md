# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community. Be constructive, assume good intent while checking facts, respect different experience levels, and keep technical disagreement focused on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, publishing private information, and repeated disruption of project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these standards and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Serious incidents should be reported privately through the repository's security reporting channel rather than discussed in a public issue.

Project maintainers are responsible for applying these standards fairly and consistently across issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.
